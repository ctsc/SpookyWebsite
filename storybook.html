HALLOWEEN CANCELED!

In an unprecedented move, Mayor Grimsworth has officially canceled Halloween celebrations throughout the town. All trick-or-treating, costume parties, and festivities are hereby banned effective immediately.

"It's for the safety of our citizens," the Mayor stated cryptically at yesterday's emergency town hall meeting. When pressed for details, he refused to elaborate further.

But locals whisper of something more sinister... Strange lights seen at the old clock tower. Pumpkins withering overnight. And an ancient curse that threatens to make this Halloween the town's last...
